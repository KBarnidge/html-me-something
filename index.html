Day 1: Dinosaur Party!

We arrived in Salt Lake City early enough to explore the city a bit, but we decided to skip that and go straight to the George S. Eccles Dinosaur Park. The park features life-size sculptures of over 100 dinosaurs, with a stunning view of the Wasatch mountains in the background.
